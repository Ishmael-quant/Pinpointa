The Problem

Many people struggle to find accurate and trustworthy information about nearby places such as schools, hospitals, restaurants, hotels, and banks. Existing platforms often provide outdated details, unclear locations, or overwhelming results that waste time.

Pinpointa is a smart location-based web platform that helps users quickly discover reliable places using maps, filters, and real user insights.

How Pinpointa Solves It

Pinpointa allows users to search for places based on their current location or any selected area. It combines interactive maps with clear visual results, images, opening hours, and user reviews to give a complete overview in one place.

Smart filters such as price range, ratings, distance, and operating hours help users narrow down results quickly and efficiently.

Why Trust Pinpointa

Pinpointa is built with transparency and accuracy as its core values. Information is continuously improved through user contributions and verified data, ensuring reliability.

By combining community input, modern design, and location technology, Pinpointa becomes a dependable tool for everyday decisions.
